Expanding Your Circle of Competence

Know what you know, know what you don't. Expand deliberately.

Charlie Munger's concept: you need to know your circle of competence. Inside: confident decisions. Outside: humility and learning.

Competence Boundaries

Overconfidence in areas you don't understand leads to poor decisions. Knowing boundaries allows focus on strengths while expanding carefully.

Know your circle. Expand it deliberately.

Managing Competence

  • Identify strengths: What do you truly know?
  • Acknowledge limits: What don't you know?
  • Expand carefully: Learn before deciding
  • Stay humble: Confidence can be misplaced

Know what you know. Know what you don't.
