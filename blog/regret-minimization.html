Regret Minimization Framework

Project yourself to end of life to clarify present priorities.

Bezos's framework: imagine yourself at 80, look back. What will you regret not doing? This perspective clarifies what truly matters now.

The 80-Year-Old View

Regret often centers on things not tried, not risks taken. The fear of embarrassment or failure fades; regret for inaction persists.

What will you regret not doing?

Applying the Framework

  • Project forward: Imagine 80-year-old you
  • Ask about regrets: What will you wish you'd done?
  • Prioritize those: Put them first now
  • Ignore small fears: They won't matter then

What will you regret not doing? Do that.
